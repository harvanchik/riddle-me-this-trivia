About Us

Cantrell brothers David and Anthony bring a combined 40 years of entertainment and performing experience to the table. Growing up, their love for games was sparked by two formative family traditions: hotly competitive Wednesday Trivial Pursuit nights and the lively Saturday Bingo Club hosted by their mother and aunt for over 35 years.

Both of these Cantrell staples provided years and years of laughter, lifelong friendships, and family unity. These weekly game nights taught them that games are more than just fun, they are a way to bring people together for a special shared experience and to make lasting memories.

David and Anthony created Riddle Me This Trivia with a mission to share that same joy and energy with others. When they host your trivia or game night, you can count on an engaging, high-energy experience that everyone will enjoy.

David Cantrell

David has been a successful businessman in the financial industry for 30 years and has held many leadership positions over the years. Most recently, he served as the President of VIA and provided strong and innovative leadership that proved to be vital for the business community of Santa Clarita and beyond.

Also, David has been a Track & Field coach for 25 years, primarily at West Ranch High School. His student athletes have always appreciated David's inspirational guidance and abundant sense of humor.

Anthony Cantrell, Ph.D.

Anthony has been a Theatre educator, actor, and director for over 40 years. He is an Adjunct Professor of Theatre at Los Angeles Pierce College and the Director of K-12 Arts Education at The Soraya, the performing arts center at Cal State, Northridge.

Anthony received his BA in Theatre from UCLA and his Ph.D. in Theatre from the University of Michigan.
